Game Instructions


  • In your turn, roll the dice at least once and accumulate the result in "current"
  • You can roll multiple times and click hold to save points and end the turn
  • If you get 6-6, you lose all points from current and the turn will go to your opponent
  • If you reach exactly the target score, you win. If you pass it, you lose
